About Scoots Rentals

Scoots is a motor scooter rental company located on Cozumel Island, off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea. They cater to locals, tourists, and cruise-based touring groups, offering friendly services of motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-side ATVs.

The company has two rental locations on the island. One is conveniently situated near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel, serving the general public. The other location is strategically positioned near the Terminal Puerta Maya, specifically catering to cruise lines and their patrons.
